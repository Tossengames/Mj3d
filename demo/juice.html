About

The short version.

What this is

Azuma Clan is a community built around real contracts — small real-world tasks members complete for points, proof, and rank. No cash, no purchases, just points earned by doing things.

How ranking works

Points come from completed contracts, daily activity, and welcoming new members. Ranks are awarded automatically as your point total climbs.

Legal

This is a hobby community project, not a company or financial product. Points have no cash value and cannot be bought, sold, or redeemed. Content submitted as proof is the responsibility of the member who posts it. The Council can remove content or members at its own discretion.
