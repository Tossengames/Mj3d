About

How the clan works, short version.

What this is

Azuma Clan is a community built around real missions — small real-world tasks members complete for points, proof, and rank. No cash, no purchases, just points earned by doing things.

How to earn points

Complete a mission5–20 pts
Daily activity+1 pt
7-day streak+5 pts
Welcome a new member+1 pt
Refer a new member+10 pts

Ranks

Ranks rise automatically as your points climb. Your rank color follows your name everywhere in the village.
