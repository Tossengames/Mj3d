💀 About the Archive

Game Graveyard exists to preserve the stories of games that were never finished. Every abandoned project contains creativity, effort, and lessons worth remembering — this is where they're kept instead of lost.

🛠

For developers

Give your unfinished work a permanent home, explain the journey, and find people who still want to see it exist.

🎮

For players

Explore canceled and unfinished games, mark them RIP or vote to revive, and dig into the stories behind them.

🗃

For everyone

A place where unfinished games don't vanish — the record stays, even if development doesn't continue.

Terms of Use Content Ownership Takedown Policy Community Conduct Privacy Disclaimer

1. Terms of Use

Game Graveyard is a free, community-run archive for unfinished, abandoned, canceled, and revived game projects. By creating an account or submitting content, you agree to these terms. The service is provided as-is, free of charge, with no guarantee of uptime, permanence, or accuracy of user-submitted content.

You must be old enough to legally agree to these terms in your country to create an account. We reserve the right to suspend or remove accounts that violate these terms.

2. Content Ownership & Your Submissions

You retain ownership of anything you submit — text, images, links, or descriptions. By submitting, you grant Game Graveyard a non-exclusive license to display that content on the site.

Every submission requires you to confirm, via checkbox, that the project is your own or that you have permission to share it. This confirmation is logged with a timestamp at the time of submission. Misrepresenting ownership is a violation of these terms and may result in account suspension.

Logged-in users can edit or permanently delete their own submissions at any time from their Dashboard.

3. Takedown Policy

If you believe content on this archive infringes your rights — including a project submitted by someone other than its rightful owner — you can request its removal.

  • Contact us using the report link on any project page, or via the contact method listed at the bottom of this page.
  • Include the project name/URL and a brief explanation of your claim.
  • We aim to review valid requests within a few business days and will remove content found to be in violation.
  • Repeat or clearly bad-faith submissions from the same account may result in permanent suspension.

This is a good-faith moderation process, not a substitute for formal legal action where that's required.

4. Community Conduct

Comments, reactions (RIP / Revive), and follows require a logged-in account so that community activity stays accountable. We ask that comments stay respectful — critique the project, not the person. Harassment, hate speech, spam, or coordinated vote manipulation may result in comment removal or account suspension.

5. Privacy

Once real accounts are implemented, we intend to collect only what's needed to operate the site: an email address and password (securely hashed, never stored in plain text) for login, and whatever profile details you choose to add. We will not sell user data to third parties or use it for advertising.

This current version of the site is a prototype — no real accounts, emails, or passwords are being stored anywhere yet.

6. Disclaimer

Game Graveyard is an independent, fan/community-run archive and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by any game studio, publisher, or platform mentioned in user submissions. Project descriptions reflect the submitting user's own account of events and may not be independently verified.
