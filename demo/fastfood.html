About

What this ledger is for

GoodWorks is a place for groups — families, classrooms, churches, teams, friends — to keep a shared record of good done. An admin adds a task. Someone takes it, does it, and shows the proof. The group confirms it happened. It goes in the book.

Points only ever build a rank inside your own group. There's no global chase, no public score to perform for — just a quiet, growing record of who showed up.
